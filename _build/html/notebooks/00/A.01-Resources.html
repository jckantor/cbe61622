Resources#

Data Acquistion, Instrumentation, and Control Companies#

  • National Instruments. Full line vendor of industrial and laboratory grade instruments and software, including LabView.

IoT Devices and Services#

  • Adafruit. Full range of devices, sensors, and software (including CircuitPython).

  • DFRobot. Producer of open source hardware and software products, including a full range of sensors, with a strong focus on developers and STEM education.

  • National Control Devices. A producer of IoT devices, software, and services for industrial and military applications. Includes feather boards for 5 volt expansion of I2C communications.

  • Particle.io. A producer of IoT devices and services with a larger developer community.

  • SeeedStudio. A producer of a IoT devices, sensors, and development kits. Produces a wide range of “Grove” sensors which use standardized connector and modules to simplify development of IoT applications.

Software Platforms#

  • Arduino

  • AWS IoT

  • Blynk. Hardware agnostic platform for creating mobile IoT applications.

  • Internet of Things (IoT) of IBM Cloud

  • Node-RED. An open-source, graphical editor for creating event-driven applications to run on Node.js. Applications can be run on Raspberry Pi or in the cloud.

  • PlatformIO.
